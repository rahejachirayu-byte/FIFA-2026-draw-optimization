# Concise Executive Summary

The optimized 4_17 revenue package now tells one clear story instead of mixing strong results with misleading ones.

What it shows now:
- The most honest core question is demand: which match characteristics increase likely attendance?
- The cleanest recurring signals are team strength, recent form, team identity, venue context, and matchup competitiveness.
- The attendance model is directionally useful but not fully deployment-ready.
- The raw revenue-proxy model looks strong mostly because stage-based ticket pricing is embedded in the target.
- Therefore, the full revenue_proxy model has been downgraded to appendix status for feature-discovery purposes.

What changed:
- The package now has a single primary specification for presentation.
- Weak artifacts were downgraded.
- The workbook was reorganized for executive use.
- The model chart and summaries were relabeled so non-technical audiences can understand them immediately.
